The rules
Rule 1 — PatienceChart pending
"Patience is the trade right now."
When price is floating in the dead zone between CPR and PDH / PDL, no entry IS the right entry. The market is undecided. Don't force it. Wait for a clean breakout candle or a clear rejection signal.
Dead zone = hands in pockets. No trade.

Rule 2 — Long triggerView chart
PDH = Gap border = Double confluence
When PDH and the gap border sit at the same level, that is double confluence. Wait for a confirmed breakout candle closing above PDH — don't anticipate. Once confirmed, shorts get squeezed and gap fill becomes the magnetic target. Don't cap gains with a spread.
Confirmed close above PDH → Naked Call · Target = Gap fill
Observed: Mar 20, 2026
Rule 3 — DisciplineChart pending
"Until the setup manifests — cash is the position."
No setup = no trade. Sitting in cash is not inaction — it is an active, deliberate decision. Waiting preserves capital and keeps you sharp for when the real opportunity appears.
No setup visible → Stay in cash. Full stop.
