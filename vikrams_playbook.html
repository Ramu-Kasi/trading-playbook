Rule 2 — Confluence Chart pending

When PDH and a gap border sit at the same level, that is double confluence resistance/support. Wait for a confirmed breakout above PDH — then go naked Call buying. Do not cap gains with a spread. The target is the gap fill.

Target GAP FILL Do not limit with a spread — let it run to gap fill

Rule 4 — Supply Zone Reversal 📊 View Chart ↗
Supply zone on Wide CPR day = High probability short.
On Wide CPR days (likely sideways or reversal), when price reaches a known supply zone, a bearish candle at that zone is a high probability short setup. A rising 20 EMA below price does NOT disqualify the trade — especially in a downtrend. Supply zone resistance overrides EMA direction.
Entry SHORT Bearish candle at supply zone on Wide CPR day
SL ABOVE ZONE Stop loss placed above the supply zone
Target CPR or 2R Take profit at CPR band or 2R — whichever comes first
Key override: Rising 20 EMA does NOT disqualify this trade. In a downtrend, supply zone resistance always overrides EMA momentum. Wide CPR = sideways/reversal bias confirmed.
